Hello participants! test your English language skill here

How this app works

  • 1 There are 10 questions in each section
  • 2 Click to Hear the Word button to listen to the word
  • 3 Type the spelling of the word you heard in the box provided
  • 4 Click Next to move to the following word
  • 5 After completing all 10 words, your final score will be displayed
    • When you are ready, press START
