Spell Check

Test your English spelling skills

How this works

  1. 1 There are 10 questions in the activity
  2. 2 Click the Hear the Word button to listen to the word
  3. 3 Type the word you heard in the box provided
  4. 4 Click Next to move to the following word
  5. 5 After completing all 10 words, your final score will be displayed

Speaking Comprehension

Read aloud and check your pronunciation

How this works

  1. 1 You will see 3 short paragraphs one at a time
  2. 2 Read the paragraph carefully, then click Record and read it aloud
  3. 3 Click Stop when you finish - your speech is checked word by word
  4. 4 Green words = correct, red words = missed or wrong. You can Try Again before moving on
  5. 5 Score 85% accuracy or above to pass a paragraph

When you are ready, press START

Typing Skill Test

How fast and accurate can you type?

How this works

  1. 1 You have 30 seconds in total
  2. 2 Type the sentence: "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog"
  3. 3 Click SUBMIT after each attempt - up to 10 attempts
  4. 4 Extra spaces are ignored, but spelling and word order must be exact
  5. 5 Your score shows how many attempts you typed correctly

When you are ready, press START
